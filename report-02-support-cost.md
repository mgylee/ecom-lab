## Support is a headcount problem

Support costs are not a software problem. They are a time problem. An agent closes a ticket. That ticket took minutes. Those minutes add up to a salary.

Most brands staff for peak volume and carry that cost year-round. They know their total support headcount. Most do not know their cost per ticket. The gap between those two numbers is where the overspend lives.

## The bottom line

Support headcount is not fixed. Every ticket AI resolves is a ticket an agent did not spend time on. At 30% automation, that is real headcount — hours that no longer need to be staffed, or capacity that absorbs volume growth without a new hire.

The brands above 30% automation are not just faster. They are cheaper to run.
